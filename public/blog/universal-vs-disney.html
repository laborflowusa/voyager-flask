The Honest Mom Calculation

If you're the one planning this trip (and data shows most moms are), you're weighing more than just ticket prices.

  • Disney means more walking, more meltdowns, and higher prices. It also means the castle moment, character hugs, and memories your kids will talk about for years.
  • Universal means better value, better rides for older kids, and Harry Potter. It also means less "magic" for the under-8 crowd.

There's no wrong answer. But knowing which one fits your family's actual budget and energy level is the difference between a great trip and a trip you regret.
